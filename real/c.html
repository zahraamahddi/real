Real Madrid

Real Madrid ( Spanish : Real Madrid Club de Fútbol, ​​which means Royal Madrid Football Club ) is a Spanish professional football team founded in 1902 , based in the Spanish capital, Madrid . The team plays in the Spanish League and was chosen as the best football team of the twentieth century . It has won the Spanish League 35 times (a record), nineteen times the King of Spain’s Cup and a record of 14 titles in the Champions League . He is also a member of the G-14 leading clubs in EuropeCurrently abolished and replaced by the European Club Association . The club emerged strongly on the European and Spanish football arena during the fifties of the twentieth century , and by the eighties of the aforementioned century, this club had one of the best sports teams in Europe , known as the " Quinta del Buitre " , which He won the European League - then the European Cup - twice in a row, five Spanish championships, the Spanish Cup once, and three Spanish Super Cups . Along with Athletic Bilbao and Barcelona , ​​it is one of only three Spanish clubs to have never been relegated to the top of Spanish football , and it is also one of the most popular clubs in the world. The club is also famous for its long-term competition with Barcelona and Atletico Madrid, where the first competition is known as El Clásico ( Spanish : El Clásico ), and the second is called the Madrid Derby ( Spanish : El Derbi madrileño ).
